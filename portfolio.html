Shubhavadhana is a student of Class XII in N S N Memorial School, Chitlapakkam, Chennai. She has been learning the nuances of Bharathanatyam in Vazhuvoor style, under the dedicated tutelage of Guru. Smt. Kanchana Janardhanan – Director, Mayura School of Classical Dances, Chennai from her tender age of three.

She has participated and performed vital roles in the dance productions like Thirupavai, Iyyappan Charithram, Saravana Bhava, Meenakshi Kalyanam and Ganesha Charithram.

She has performed at various stages and events organized by Mayura School of Classical Dances and also under the banners of various Sabhas. She is also undergoing a Certification Programme in Bharathanatyam from Annamalai Univeristy and have cleared Grade I level in Distinction.

She also practices Carnatic vocal music and Veena under the able guidance of Guru Smt. Indhumathi and actively participates in many programmes. At this tender age, she has displayed the potential of being an all-rounder by not only excelling in academics, but also actively pursuing extra-curricular activities. Her interest in drawing, painting and fine arts has won her laurels in school competitions. She has also shown tremendous potential as a Chess player by representing in District level tournaments and has won many prizes.

A multi-talented child is also an active member as a “Group Patrol” of her school’s Scouts & Guides team and a “Rajpuraskar” awardee from the Tamil Nadu Government. To add more, she has completed UCMAS – Abacus & Hindi language proficiency exams from Dakshin Bharat Hindi Prachar Sabha.
